Part 1¶

I have selected three models that were trained on the 'spambase' dataset. These models include Linear Regression, LightGBM, and TabFPN. To compare the results of these models, I used LIME on three randomly chosen observations.

After analyzing the results, I found that there were a lot of similarities in the most important features for all three models. Surprisingly, all of the models regarded 'word_freq_goerge' as one of the most important features. However, LGBM and TabFPN also placed a lot of weight on 'word_freq_hp'. I originally thought that 'capital_run_length_average' would be more important, but it appeared only once in the analysis of the selected observations.
